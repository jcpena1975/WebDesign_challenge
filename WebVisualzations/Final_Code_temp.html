Latitude vs Temperature

According to the plotted example shown, we can see that temperature is can be affected by the latitude. The closer to the equator, the warmer the temperature. We also have to keep in mind the time of year data was retrieved. If the data is taken during the summer months, we have to acknowledge that warmer temperatures are to be expected.
